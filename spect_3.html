Wild Circus presents O

Experience an aquatic masterpiece

  Weaving a tapestry of artistry, surrealism and theatrical romance, O pays tribute to the beauty of theatre.
  Inspired by the concept of infinity and the elegance of water, world-class acrobats, synchronized swimmers and divers create a breathtaking experience.

 

Aerial Hoops
Dizzying choreography and powerful gymnastics in the air and on the water.
Boat
A ghostly ship emerges from the mist. Flyers and catchers display incredible timing.
Frame
A herd of aquatic zebras swoop and swing on a giant frame in a surreal water dance.
Fire
The rage of fire and the purification of water are blended into one ... and then displayed in this brilliantly visual act.
High Dive
A group of three world class high divers leap 60 feet from the grid level to a small exposed section of the pool, without any fear or hesitation.
Underwater
All of the 150 technicians and 80 cast members have scuba certification. 14 technicians work underwater during every performance.

 

O

O defies description, as spectacular and inventive a live show as you‘re likely to find anywhere in the world.
